Relationships & Me

A Relationships can be like raging seas or serene lakes. It is all up to one, how they choose to identify and respond to the situations. This is important to keep oneself safe and happy.

Humans have evolved from an insignificant species to the most advanced species on this planet because of social interaction. These interactions have built relationships and communities. But how does one know if the way they are involved in socializing with someone is formative, beneficial, or even safe? The backbone of this webinar was to clarify these questions and more in social interactions. To recognize and respect various boundaries and understand the red flags in relationships was one of the few highlights of this webinar.
