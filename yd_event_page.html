International Youth Day

On the occasion of International Youth Day, TIE Club along with Rotaract Club TSEC wanted to emphasise on the role our elders play in our lives. We asked our dearest grandparents to send in some words of wisdom for today’s youth and enlighten them with what they’ve learnt throughout their lives.

On the occasion of International Youth Day, TIE Club along with Rotaract Club TSEC wanted to emphasise on the role the elders play in the lives of the youth. We asked our beloved grandparents to send in videos to enlighten and remind the youth with what is actually important in our lives and what they should really be focusing on. We also asked them to advice us with what the youth should or shouldn’t be doing at various stages of life. This activity reassured us about some aspects of our lives, enlightening the youth with what the elderly have learnt throughout their lives and helping them tackle situations in the future.
