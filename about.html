About Us

Objectives and Goals

TIE Club was founded in 2018 with a Mission to propagate the AKA of Mental Health: Increasing Awareness that Mental Health issues exist, improving Knowledge on what these issues are & how to cope with them and providing Access to professional help.

The Goal was to create a conducive environment for discussion without judgement, for promoting a solution-oriented rather than problem-oriented approach. In the 2 years of operation so far, we have succeeded in reaching out to students one event at a time and creating the very kind of environment we were aiming for.
